When Boss AMAD was established, the company's goal was to create uncomplicated, easy-to-wear clothing that had an old feel. We use zigzag designs, vibrant colors, and even animals to help us achieve the simplicity we desire. We don't have to depict angel wings or heartstrings in excruciating detail. We want you to envision yourself in a dimly lit room, listening to a jazz performer play the horn. In contrast, The History Clothing Company aims to instill enthusiasm. We want you to foot on a burning piece of asphalt with the sound of a boom box playing in the background.

    Our main objective is to make our neighborhood and environment better. In the garment industry, we aim to do this by coming up with appealing designs. We design our apparel with the intention of encouraging individuals to slow down, appreciate the better things in life, and express themselves.
